Privacy Policy.

Effective Date: March 2026 · Last Updated: 28 March 2026

TL;DR: We collect your email if you sign up, basic analytics to improve the site, and nothing else. No tracking across sites, no selling data, no purchase history. Ever.

FairVein ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at fairvein.com (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address — only if you voluntarily sign up for our waitlist or newsletter
  • Search queries — brand names you search on the site (not linked to your identity)

Information collected automatically:

  • Basic analytics — page views, referral source, general geographic region, device type, and browser type
  • User agent string — sent with email signups to help us understand which devices our users prefer

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what we don't do:

  • We do not track your browsing history across other websites
  • We do not collect purchase data or shopping behavior
  • We do not use cross-site tracking pixels or retargeting
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not build advertising profiles based on your usage
  • We do not collect financial or payment information (the Service is free)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Email addresses — to send you product updates, launch notifications, and occasional newsletters. You can unsubscribe at any time.
  • Analytics data — to understand how people use FairVein, improve the user experience, and prioritize which brands to add to our database.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process limited data:

  • Convex — cloud database used to store email signups. Convex Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — hosts our website. GitHub may collect standard server logs. GitHub Privacy Statement
  • TikTok API — used by our editorial team to publish educational content to the @fairvein TikTok account. No end-user data is shared with TikTok through our integration. TikTok Privacy Policy

We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party advertising trackers.

5. Cookies

FairVein uses minimal cookies:

  • Session storage — to remember if you've already seen the email signup prompt during your current visit (expires when you close the browser)

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking cookies, or persistent cookies for profiling. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting core site functionality.

6. Data Retention

  • Email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Analytics data — aggregated and anonymized; raw logs retained for no more than 90 days

7. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, we respect the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Data portability — request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from emails at any time via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@fairvein.com. We will respond within 30 days.

GDPR Note: If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your email is consent (you opted in). For analytics, our legal basis is legitimate interest in improving our Service.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure cloud infrastructure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

FairVein is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@fairvein.com.

10. International Data Transfers

Our Service is operated from Singapore. If you access the Service from outside Singapore, your information may be transferred to and processed in Singapore or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. If we make material changes, we will notify subscribers via email.

12. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, contact us at:

privacy@fairvein.com

General inquiries: hello@fairvein.com
