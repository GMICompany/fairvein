## Week 2 Content Notes

### Theme Progression
- Week 1: Estée Lauder (beauty-specific, boycott-driven)
- Week 2: L'Oréal + Unilever + LVMH (broader corporate consolidation story)
- The narrative arc goes from "one company is bad" → "the ENTIRE industry is consolidated"

### Key Differences from Week 1
- Post 5 (Dove vs Axe) introduces **hypocrisy angle** — strongest emotional trigger
- Post 6 (LVMH) introduces **wealth inequality angle** — $170B billionaire
- Both expand beyond beauty into lifestyle brands (Ben & Jerry's, Louis Vuitton)

### Analytics Expectations
- Post 5 should be the top performer (hypocrisy content goes viral)
- Post 6 has Rihanna/Fenty tie-in (celebrity hooks boost reach)
- Post 4 is the "informational" baseline — important but less emotional

### If Week 1 Data Shows:
- **Views > 10K:** Keep current format (6 slides, text-heavy)
- **Views < 5K:** Test shorter hooks (3-4 slides), punchier text
- **High views but low clicks:** Rework slide 6 CTA — make fairvein.com larger
- **High shares:** Double down on "shock reveal" format

- Slide images: Product shots from Pexels/Unsplash (same as Week 1)
