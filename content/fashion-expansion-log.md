## Boycott Quality Control

**Total boycotts added:** 6  
**Criteria applied:**
- ✅ Well-documented with credible sources (Business & Human Rights, BDS Movement, NGOs)
- ✅ Ongoing campaigns with news coverage
- ✅ Specific, verifiable claims (not vague accusations)
- ❌ Historical issues resolved years ago (excluded)
- ❌ Minor controversies without sustained campaigns (excluded)
- ❌ Allegations without independent verification (excluded)

**Sources used:**
- Business & Human Rights Resource Centre (Uyghur forced labor report)
- BDS Movement official campaigns (Puma, Adidas)
- SaveUighur.org documentation
