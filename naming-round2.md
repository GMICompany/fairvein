# EthicalCart Naming - Round 2
**Date:** 2026-03-27  
**Mission:** Find available .com domains under $20 for supply chain transparency tool

## Strategy
Following David Placek's naming framework:
- **Surprising + Familiar** compound words
- **Avoided:** Obvious descriptors (ethical+X, brand+X, shop+X)
- **Used:** Latin roots, metaphors, action verbs, nature/geography concepts
- **Focus:** Less-common prefixes/suffixes, unexpected combinations

## Verification Method
1. HEAD request to https://[domain].com
2. Check DNS resolution
3. Test robots.txt endpoint
4. Timeout patterns indicate potential parking/availability

## Naming Framework Analysis

**What worked:**
- Vein/thread/path metaphors (biological/network imagery)
- Axis/fork/root (structural/navigation concepts)
- Avoiding obvious ethical/brand/shop prefixes

**What was taken:**
- Almost all -mark, -trace, -line combinations
- Most true-/clear-/pure- prefixes
- Simple two-syllable compounds

**Insight:** The sweet spot is **unexpected biological or structural metaphors** combined with **action/navigation concepts**. "Forkwise" succeeds because fork isn't an obvious transparency word, but it perfectly captures the decision-making aspect.
